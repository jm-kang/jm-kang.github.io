About

Hello! I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech advised by Alan Ritter and Wei Xu. Previously, I completed my M.S. in Computer Science at KAIST, where I started my NLP journey. I had a wonderful time interning at MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab.

My research focuses on developing NLP models that are efficient and robust, with the goal of ensuring their practicality in real-world scenarios.

  • How to induce NLP systems to be more scalable and cheaper in terms of data, compute, or parameters?
  • How to design NLP models that remain robust when confronted with unseen cases in the wild?

In particular, below are some keywords of my recent interests:

  • Large Language Models
  • Instruction-Driven Learning
  • Retrieval-Augmentation
  • Efficient NLP
  • Robustness
